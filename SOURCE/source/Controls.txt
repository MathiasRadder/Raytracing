Controls:
Camera controls:
Like unreal engine.
Mouse:
- Rotate Yaw (RMB + Mouse Move X) 
- Rotate Pitch (RMB + Mouse Move Y) 
- Move (local) Forward/Backward (LMB + Mouse Move Y) 
-  Rotate Yaw (LMB + Mouse Move X) 
-  Move (world) Up/Down/Left/Right (MMB) 

Keyboard:
- Move (local) Forward (Arrow Up) and (W) 
- Move (local) Backward (Arrow Down) and (S) 
- Move (local) Right (Arrow Right) and (D) 
- Move (local) Left (Arrow Left) and (A) 

Rendering:
Keyboard:
- Toggle Shadows ON/OFF (Q) 
- Toggle Light-Equation Terms (E) 
- enable/disable directional light(J)
- enable/disable left-right point light(K-L)
- Switch scene (G)

Press T for controls.
